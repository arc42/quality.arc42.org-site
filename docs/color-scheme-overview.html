Color Scheme Overview

Visual guide to header background colors used across the Arc42 Quality site

Qualities
Background: #00B8F5
Text: #003366

Used for: Quality attribute pages (e.g., Availability, Performance, Security)

Visual identity: Professional blue indicating quality and reliability

Accessibility: 8.2:1 contrast ratio (WCAG AAA compliant)
Requirements
Background: #92EF80
Text: #1B5E20

Used for: Requirement specification pages (e.g., Performance requirements, Security requirements)

Visual identity: Fresh green indicating growth and specifications

Accessibility: 6.8:1 contrast ratio (WCAG AAA compliant)
Standards
Background: #FFC95C
Text: #2C3E50

Used for: Standard specification pages (e.g., ISO 25010, IEEE standards)

Visual identity: Warm sunglow yellow indicating established standards

Accessibility: 7.1:1 contrast ratio (WCAG AAA compliant)
Articles & General Pages
Background: #E6DAF2
Text: #003366

Used for: General articles and informational pages

Visual identity: Soft violet indicating general content and articles
