Stimulus: A new release of an authentication/authorization service (which our product uses) is made available in the component marketplace, and the product owner decides to incorporate this version into the release.

Response: The new service is tested and deployed to the production environment.

Measure: It takes less than 40 hours of elapsed time and no more than 120 person-hours of effort. The deployment introduces no defects and no SLA is violated.
