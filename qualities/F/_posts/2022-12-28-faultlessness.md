Definitions:

>
>[ISO-25010:2023](/references/#iso-25010-2023)

<hr class="with-no-margin"/>

>The term “faultlessness” replaces the previous name “maturity (in [ISO-25010-2011](/references/#iso-25010-2011)) as quality subcharacteristic.
>
>[ISO-25010:2023](/references/#iso-25010-2023)
