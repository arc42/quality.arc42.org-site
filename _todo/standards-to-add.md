# Standards to Add (Prioritized)

## Priority 1
- ISO 9001 (quality management systems) — baseline for organizational quality assurance and continual improvement across industries.
- ISO/IEC 20000-1 (IT service management systems) — standard for consistent service quality and lifecycle control.
- ISO/IEC 27002 (information security controls) — practical control guidance that complements ISO/IEC 27001.
- ISO/IEC 27701 (privacy information management) — extends 27001/27002 to govern PII handling and privacy quality.
- ISO 22301 (business continuity management) — resilience and continuity of quality during disruptions.

## Priority 2
- ISO/IEC/IEEE 15288 (system life cycle processes) — widely used to ensure systems engineering quality across lifecycle stages.
- ISO 31000 (risk management guidelines) — cross-industry standard for risk-based quality planning and decisions.
- ISO/IEC 25012 (data quality model) — critical as product quality depends on data quality and data-driven behavior.
- ISO/IEC 29110 (very small entities profiles) — quality guidance tailored for small teams and SMEs.

## Priority 3
- ISO/SAE 21434 (automotive cybersecurity engineering) — global reference for connected vehicle software/system quality.
- ISO 13485 (medical devices quality management) — essential for regulated safety-critical software/firmware contexts.
