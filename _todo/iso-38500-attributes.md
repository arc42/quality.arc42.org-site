# Quality Attributes Addressed by ISO/IEC 38500:2024

## Directly addressed (strong governance link)

- **governability** — Core focus of the standard; Evaluate–Direct–Monitor model
- **compliance** — Conformance principle; adherence to laws, regulations, policies
- **accountability** — Responsibility principle; clear roles and decision authority
- **auditability** — Monitor task; measurable evidence of performance and conformance
- **transparency** — Acquisition/Strategy principles; transparent, evidence-based decisions
- **security** — Cybersecurity governance strengthened in 2024 edition
- **sustainability** — 2024 edition; environmental impact of IT operations
- **risk-identification** — Evaluate task; assessing risks from IT use

## Indirectly addressed (governance enables these)

- **traceability** — Tracking decisions and outcomes to objectives
- **performance** — Performance principle; IT service quality levels
- **controllability** — Direct task; setting policies and plans
- **operability** — Performance principle; delivering operational IT services
- **data-protection** — Conformance principle; compliance with privacy regulations
- **data-sovereignty** — Conformance principle; adherence to data jurisdiction laws
- **maintainability** — Strategy principle; sustainable, long-term IT planning
- **reliability** — Performance principle; ensuring service continuity
- **fairness** — Human Behaviour principle; ethical IT decisions
- **accessibility** — Human Behaviour principle; considering needs of all affected people
- **energy-efficiency** — Sustainability focus in 2024 edition; eco-friendly IT
