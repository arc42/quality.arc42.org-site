<div class="quality-requirement" markdown="1">

All parts of the system shall be usable by people suffering color blindnesses:

* Deuteranomaly 
* Protanomaly 
* Protanopia 
* Deuteranopia
* Tritanomaly
* Tritanopia and
* complete color blindness
