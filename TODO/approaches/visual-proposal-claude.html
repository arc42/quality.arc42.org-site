Alternative B

Dimension deep-dive — sticky sidebar + scrollable dimension sections

A single page: a sticky sidebar lists all 9 dimensions for quick navigation; the main area shows each dimension as an expandable section (like the tag pages — reliable, secure, etc.) with a short description and a compact row-list of its approaches. A search input in the sidebar filters approach names inline. No page navigation needed — great for discovery and comparison across dimensions.
